About GroceryCart

GroceryCart is an Indian online grocery platform focused on speed, reliability and fresh produce. We partner with local vendors to bring nearby markets to your doorstep so you can get fresh fruits, vegetables and household essentials without compromise.

We designed our operations to be fast and dependable. Orders are packed carefully and delivered by trained partners with strict quality checks. Our technology helps vendors manage inventory efficiently while offering customers the freshest selection every day.

GroceryCart blends the convenience of modern e-commerce with the trust and local touch of neighbourhood markets. Our aim is to make grocery shopping effortless and enjoyable for urban customers across cities we operate in.

Quick facts

  • Founded: 2025
  • Operating cities: Delhi NCR, Bengaluru, Mumbai, Pune
  • Average delivery time: Under 25 minutes
